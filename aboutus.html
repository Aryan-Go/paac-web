Who We Are

The PAAC Initiative is a vibrant hub fostering scientific discourse, primarily focused on physics, astronomy, and computational sciences. We aim to promote research and collaboration among enthusiasts and professionals.

Our Mission

Our mission is to spark curiosity and exploration by creating opportunities for learning and research in astrophysics and related fields, encouraging discussions and hands-on experience.

Our Activities

  • Organizing guest lectures, workshops, and interactive sessions on astrophysics.
  • Providing a platform for research collaboration and participation in global competitions.
  • Engaging in computational astrophysics and contributing to the scientific community.
